About Us

My name is Anthony, and I am a student studying Interactive Media Design at Algonquin College. I specialize in UX design, web design, and web development, where I have utilized some of those skills to create this website using HTML/CSS and Bootstrap. Additionally, I am proficient in digital graphics, Photoshop, photography, video production, and more.

Space is so much more than just a black sky with white dots. It's a boundless canvas filled with the most beautiful and colorful sights that we can imagine. That's why my mission is to create my portfolio built around a space theme. I want to showcase all the beautiful colors, planets, galaxies, and more. I also want to add the relationship between humans and space such as rockets, astronauts, and even sci-fi art styles.
